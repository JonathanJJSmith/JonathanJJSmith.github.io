Jonathan Smith

Hi! I'm Jonathan Smith, a 15 year old sophomore at Palm Desert High School. My biggest passions are video game development, film production, and digital art. Academics-wise, I want to get a degree that can get me into video game development (All fields of that area like graphics, programming, and music). Video games have always inspired me. Another thing that inspires me is the "behind-the-scenes" of the video game. So I wanted to know how video games work and that is why I got into that field.

I sought my other interest of film/broadcasting and attempted to begin a career on YouTube. I was somewhat successful and I still do this passion as a hobby to this very day (not for monetary reasons yet). I am also a really good artist (in both painting and music). I have painted plenty of pictures and composed many songs. Some of my greatest works include a custom box art cover for Megaman 10 and an original song called Once. I still do these art and music hobbies to this very day as well.
