IcelanDict Demo

IcelanDict is a translator and dictionary app that was created as a way to make learning Icelandic an easier and more enjoyable experience. With every word you discover, it adds more knowledge to your index!

The branding screen, menu, translator, and index functionalities are demonstrated in the video.

Whenever you translate a word from English into Icelandic, the Icelandic word is saved into the index.

You can try out this program for yourself here!
